# Remote Access to the Workstations

 [NIC Analysis
 workstations](NIC-Analysis-workstations.html)

## To get remote access, you must email Kari Herrington (Kari.herrington@ucsf.edu) 


## Remember, all the time on workstations must be booked!


## To access workstations remotely after you have been granted access.


1. Go to
 [https://remoteaccess.ucsf.edu/console](https://remoteaccess.ucsf.edu/console)
2. Select Authenticate Using SAML Credentials → Log In
    - This will take you to a page where you will use your My Access
 Log in
3. On the Left will be the Jump Groups, select \"UCSF-CALM Remote\"
4. Then there will be a list of computers to jump to, select the
 workstation you have booked, and on the right view plane, click
 Jump!



## Tips

VPN will lead to remote access NOT working.

Pay attention to the top icons to have higher quality. and be able to
see the actual size of the screen!
