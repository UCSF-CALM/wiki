# Fiji- Viewing orthagonal slices

1. Orthoganol slices allow you to view your z stacks from the slide!
2. In Fiji OPen your image
3. First set the colors and brightness and contrast for each image
4. Color → on Channel on channel bar → go to LUT button and
 choose channel
5. Brightness contrast- Image → Adjust → Brightness and
 Contrast
 ![Brightness and Contrast settings]({{ '/assets/img/DataAnalysis/ortogonal views/701468063.png' | relative_url }})
6. Image→Stacks→ Orthagonal views
![Orthogonal views menu]({{ '/assets/img/DataAnalysis/ortogonal views/701468064.png' | relative_url }})
7. Move around the cross hairs to see the different regions of the z
 sections
 ![Orthogonal view result]({{ '/assets/img/DataAnalysis/ortogonal views/701468065.png' | relative_url }})
