## **[FP BASE](https://www.fpbase.org/microscope/iqvwVz3wMgzWv7LTmVaUUC/){.external-link rel="nofollow"}** 

[This a super-resolution spinning disk confocal that can provide a 1.5
fold improvement in resolution over a standard spinning disk confocal.
It is capable of imaging at video-rate speeds with a Photometrics
Kinetex camera and is equipped with a Mightex DMD and a CO2 and
temperature-controlled incubator for live-cell
imaging.]

### Objectives:

- HC PL FL 10x/0.30
- HC PL APO 20x/0.80
- HC PL APO 40x/0.95 CORR
- HCX PL APO 40x/1.10 W CORR
- HC PL APO 100x/1.47 OIL CORR TIRF

\

### Equipment Details:

- Leica DMi8 Inverted Microscope
- Yokagawa CSU-W1 SoRA
  - 50um disk
  - SoRa disk
  - SoRa Mag 1x, 2.8x, 4.0x
  - Zt405/488/561/640 dichroic and 100%mirror
- Yokogawa Uniformizer
- Photometrics Kinetix camera
- Photometrics Prime BSI camera
- Mightex Spatial Illuminator
- Microvolution Deconvolution Software
- ASI motorized stage with Piezo top plate (500 um travel)
- OkoLab Cage Incubator
