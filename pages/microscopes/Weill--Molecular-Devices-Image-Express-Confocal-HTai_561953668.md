This system is a high-content imaging system providing both spinning
disk confocal and widefield imaging. It is optimized for imaging
multi-well plates and has both air and water-immersion objectives. It
also has temperature and CO2 for live-cell imaging.

\

### Equipment Details:

- ImageXpress Confocal HT. ai High-Content Imaging System
- Andor Zyla 4.5 camera
- Dual disk unit allowing selection between 3 modes A) 60 micron pinhole
  disk for your basic confocal requirements B) 50 micron slit for high
  throughput confocal requirements and C) wide field (not-confocal)
  imaging mode.
- Gas Mixer
- MetaXpress Imaging and Analysis software

# **Reccomendations** 

### **To know when you get started** 

- Knowing your plate dimensions is critical! If you have specific
  information about your plate it will make the autofocusing easier and
  smoother
- An empty plate is also important for measuing the dimensions of the
  plate for autofocusing
