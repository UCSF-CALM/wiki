[This is a super-resolution laser-scanning confocal outfitted with 3
STED depletion lasers. It is capable of standard laser-scanning confocal
microscopy, STED, tau-STED, and FLIM. With the White-Light Laser (WLL)
it can be used to image a wide variety of fluorophores and is capable of
separating fluorophores spectrally
close.]

### Hardware 

- Leica DMi8 Inverted Microscope
- Leica Adaptive Focus Control (AFC)
- Scanning stage SCAN plus inverted motorized stage
- Z-Piezo stage with 500um travel (WieneckeSinske WSBPiezo)
- Water Immersion micro dispenser
- 8kHz Tandem Scanner STELLARIS 8 (galvo and resonant)
- HyD detectors
  - HyD S (pos 1 and pos 3)
  - HyD X (pos 2 and pos 4)
  - HyD R (pos 5)

\

### **Light Source** 

- Leica LED 3 (390-680nm) with 3mm LLG
