### Optics 

#### Objectives 

- Zeiss W Plan-Apochromat water dipping objective, 20x/1.0NA, 2.4mm
  working distance

#### Emission for 2-photon:

- 612/69 (PMT 1)
- 525/40 (PMT 2)

#### Emission Filters for Spinning Disk Confocal:

- 617/73
- 525/30

#### Emission filters for Widefield:

- GFP cube

- Texas Red cube
