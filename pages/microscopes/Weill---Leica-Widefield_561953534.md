[This is a conventional widefield system for both epifluorescence and
brightfield imaging and is equipped with CO2 and a
temperature-controlled incubator for live-cell imaging. The fully
automated system enables large image acquisition and multi-position high
throughput imaging of plates. It is equipped with a Hamamatsu Flash 4.0
camera and a Leica color camera. Leica Thunder software module is also
available on this
system.]

### Objectives:

- N PLAN 5x/0.12 PH0
- HC PL APO 20x/0.80
- HC PL APO 40x/0.95 CORR
- HC PL APO 63x/1.40-0.60 OIL

\

### Equipment Details:

- Leica DMi8 Inverted Microscope
- Quantum HS linear motorized stage (Pecan)
- Hamamatsu Flash 4.0 v3
- Leica DFC9000 GTC color camera
- OkoLab Cage Incubator
- Thunder Software Package
