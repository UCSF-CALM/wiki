This is a manual fluorescence stereo microscope that can be used for
checking samples. It is equipped with a Leica K5 camera.

\

### Objectives:

- Plan Apo 1x Corr. Objective
- Plan Apo 2x Corr. Objective

\

### Equipment Details:

- TL5000 Ergo Stereo Microscopy

\

### Light Source:

- Leica LED 3 (390-680nm) with 3mm LLG

### Filters:

- ET BFP - M205FA/M165FC filter cube
- ET CY5 - M205FA/M165FC filter cube
- ET mCherry - M205FA/M165FC filter cube
