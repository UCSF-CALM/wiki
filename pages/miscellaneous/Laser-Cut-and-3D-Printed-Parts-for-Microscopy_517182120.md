# Laser Cut and 3D Printed Parts for Microscopy

Here are 3D parts we have designed for microscopes. Most have been
designed in either Solidworks or Autocad Inventor ([which can be
obtained free for academic
use](http://www.autodesk.com/education/schools-educators-software "http://www.autodesk.com/education/schools-educators-software")).

#### 96-well format sample holders

These are stage inserts with the same footprint as a standard 96-well
plate, so they will sit in any 96-well plate holder.
